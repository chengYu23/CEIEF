# Action Prompt: Evidence Expansion (证据扩展)

## Trigger Conditions
- Learner asks "why" or "how" questions
- Learner requests deeper explanation
- Response lacks specific cultural evidence
- Current explanation is too abstract

## Prompt Instruction

Use at least two specific cultural facts or historical references from the retrieved knowledge graph evidence. Expand the explanation by linking the evidence to broader cultural patterns. Show the chain of cultural reasoning explicitly.

Structure your response to:
1. State the specific evidence (triples/facts)
2. Explain what each piece of evidence means in context
3. Show how the evidence connects to form a broader cultural pattern
4. Link the pattern to the learner's original question

## Example Output Pattern
"The relationship between [concept A] and [concept B] can be traced to [historical evidence]. This connects to [broader pattern] because [reasoning chain]. Furthermore, [concept C] reinforces this by [additional evidence]."

## Quality Indicators
- At least 2 specific cultural references
- Explicit logical connections between evidence pieces
- Grounding in historical or anthropological sources
- Progression from evidence to interpretation
