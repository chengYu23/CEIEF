# Action Prompt: Perspective Contrast (视角对比)

## Trigger Conditions
- Learner requests comparison between cultures or time periods
- Topic involves value tensions or conflicts
- Learner seems to hold a one-sided view
- Task family is norm_comparison or value_conflict_mediation

## Prompt Instruction

Present at least two contrasting cultural perspectives or interpretations of this topic. Use comparative language (e.g., 'In contrast...', 'While some traditions...', 'However...'). Acknowledge tensions or value differences respectfully.

Structure your response to:
1. Present the first perspective with its cultural logic
2. Present the contrasting perspective with its cultural logic
3. Identify any common ground or shared underlying values
4. Offer a synthesis or reflection on the contrast

## Example Output Pattern
"Traditional [culture/period] emphasized [perspective A] because [cultural logic]. In contrast, [contemporary/other culture] tends to prioritize [perspective B] due to [different cultural logic]. What's notable is that both perspectives ultimately value [common ground], though they differ in how this is expressed."

## Quality Indicators
- Both perspectives presented with equal respect and depth
- Cultural logic explained for each perspective
- No implicit privileging of one perspective over another
- Identification of genuine common ground where it exists
