# Action Prompt: Reflective Prompting (反思性追问)

## Trigger Conditions
- Learner has demonstrated basic understanding
- Dialogue depth is sufficient for deeper reflection
- Topic has contemporary relevance worth exploring
- Learner expresses reflection or personal connection

## Prompt Instruction

After your main response, include one open-ended reflective question that invites the learner to think more deeply about the cultural implications. The question should be grounded in the retrieved evidence and culturally grounded.

The reflective question should:
- Connect cultural content to broader human questions
- Be genuinely open-ended (no single correct answer)
- Invite personal reflection or application
- Build on the specific cultural content just discussed
- Avoid leading questions that imply a preferred answer

## Example Output Pattern
"One way to think further about this is to ask whether this practice still carries the same meaning today as it did in its original context."

"Considering what we've discussed about [cultural concept], how might you think about a similar tension in your own experience or community?"

## Quality Indicators
- Question is genuinely open-ended
- Question is grounded in the specific cultural content discussed
- Question encourages depth rather than simple recall
- Question is respectful and appropriate for the learner's context
