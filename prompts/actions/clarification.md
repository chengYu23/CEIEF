# Action Prompt: Clarification (澄清简化)

## Trigger Conditions
- Learner expresses confusion or asks for clarification
- Learner uses short, unclear, or repetitive inputs
- Confusion level assessed as HIGH
- Previous response may have been too complex

## Prompt Instruction

Simplify your explanation by breaking it into 2-3 clear points. Define any technical cultural terms before using them. Use concrete, relatable examples to anchor abstract concepts. Check for understanding at the end.

Structure:
1. Acknowledge the complexity and offer to clarify
2. Define key terms simply
3. Explain the concept in plain language with a concrete example
4. Check understanding with a simple question

## Example Output Pattern
"Let me break this down more clearly. [Term] simply means [plain definition]. Think of it this way: [concrete everyday example]. Does this help clarify the concept?"

## Quality Indicators
- All technical terms defined before use
- At least one concrete real-world example
- Language accessible to non-specialists
- Understanding check included
